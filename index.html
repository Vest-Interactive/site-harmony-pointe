About

Experience the tranquil charm of small town living, with award-winning schools, ample shopping, and entertainment close at hand.

Situated in the rolling green hills just outside Crestwood, Kentucky, Brentwood offers the best of country living, with the modern conveniences a growing family desires.

Amenities

With over 41 acres of designated greenspace, Brentwood presents a relaxed, small town atmosphere. And modern amenities such as a planned clubhouse, pool, and tennis courts reflect the comfortable lifestyle of Oldham County. Brentwood offers features and conveniences that will make you want to call it home.

Plus, you have the added convenience of Oldham County elementary, middle, and high schools nearby, off of Highway 329.
